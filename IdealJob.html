Ideal Job


Oracle DBA / Cloud AWS Data Operations
Screenshot of job posting

The above position is for a database specialist that can help with analysing data for multiple areas within the company. This role also calls for someone that can communicate well with others, and can also self-manage their workload. This role is appealing to me as I’m a very analytic person that loves the idea of working within a database management type career path.


I believe below are the skills/qualifications/experience required for this role:

    -Exposure to the Oracle database management system

    -Administration & data recording

    -Experience with –

        -Linux and Windows Server

        -AWS (Amazon Web Services)

    -Strong people skills with the ability to communicate with management and higher ups


The current skills that I have which would be transferable to this role are as below:

    -Strong communication with the ability to convey information to customers, low level staff and management

    -Great time management

    -Ability to work independently and as a part of a team

    -Problem solving complaints that customers/staff may have

    -Improving processes to make things more streamlined and efficient


The way I plan to gain better skills and qualifications to progress towards this career path is to do any free courses/certifications that I can with companies such as Microsoft, Google and Amazon (AWS). I am also looking into starting to do freelance work to broaden my skills within the IT sector (specifically within data input and database management), this will allow me to build up a portfolio of skills/experience while also allowing me to further my communication skills when dealing with a wide variety of people from different sectors.
