Project Idea - "Cal"


Motto - The next one is on Cal


My project would be developing a mobile based application that will show what bottle shops and pubs are open around you based on either your current location (as a default) or the search location/suburb that you input. The application would use information that has been harvested from Google maps and use a traffic light system on their identifying pin drops to easily see their current status based on opening hours.


My motivation for the creation of this app is to make it easier on a night out when you are looking for the next bar/pub/club to go to. It is also to identify bottle shops that are open if you are about to head out to a friends place, or if you are finished with work/going out and about to head home. One of my favourite things to do is get together with friends for a drink at a local bar, or grab a few bottles of wine before going to their place. I am also an inherently lazy person, so I sometimes get frustrated with the clunky layout of Google maps when trying to locate an open venue, then having to click on it and read their open hours. This application help simplify this and save time.


This application would be first designed as a mobile based application, as I believe that would be the main device people would use it on. The main feature of it would be the traffic light system.

This would be as follows:


Red – Closed


Amber – Open, but will be closed in less than a hour


Green – Open for the next hour or longer


The traffic light system would be applied to the pin drop of each venue which would show up on the map. When you click onto a pin drop that you are interested in, a little dialogue box would pop out next to it showing some more details on that specific location.


This dialogue box will display the key information for that venue – business name, opening hours for that day, what that venue is (bottle shop, bar, pub or club) and the physical address.


If you wish to know more about the business, you would click on the business name within the box. This would then minimise the map display to a small portion at the top of the screen and more details for the selected venue would be displayed. In this detailed section it would list the business name, opening hours across all days of the week and physical address. Below that it would then list the phone number, website, star rating based on reviews.
