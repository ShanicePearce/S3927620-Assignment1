Interest in IT


I am interested in IT as I believe it is the most stable future career, and is also a career that I can do while I travel.


My interest in IT began when we got the first family computer when I was 7 years old, and I spent HOURS playing Raymond and mucking around with the original Microsoft Suite. I then grew up using MSN and sprucing up my Bebo and MySpace accounts by altering the HTML coding. I let this interest slide by in favour of working in an industry that got me decent money at the time, but wish I had of stuck with it and made that my focus.


I chose to study with RMIT through OUA as it has a great reputation within Victoria, and I wish to study my full degree with RMIT once I decided what course to take and have enough credit built up. I believe that this introductory course is a great way to get back into study after 10 years, and also get a feel for how RMIT operates as well before jumping into a full-time course load.


During this 3 month period I expect that the introductory course will be a great way of learning the fundamentals of Information Technology and also help me learn my strengths and weaknesses. Once this course is complete, I will then have a much better idea of what form of IT I would like to further study in as well.
