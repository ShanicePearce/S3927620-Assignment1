Thoughts on the testing


Looking at the above test results, I think that it reflects that I am a very goal driven person. Once I start a task, I always like to finish it to the best of my abilities.


I believe that these results show that in a team environment, I am more likely to be the person thinking about the plan and how to implement it. I’m not usually the person leading the conversation; however, I will be the person that make sure the task gets done to the best of our abilities.


When forming a team, I don’t generally look at people’s personalities. I tend to more look at their proven body of work when possible, and if they can be respectful of other people’s opinions.


No one will ever 100% get along, but if they get the job done, I am happy. That could also just be me wanting to avoid conflict whenever possible.


When it comes to these personality tests, I don’t put a lot of weight behind them. As someone that has autism, I know firsthand that one test doesn't suit all. The idea of standardised testing to "See who someone is" doesn't sit well with me.
